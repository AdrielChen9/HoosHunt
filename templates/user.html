How to Play:
  1. Choose your Bundle

    We have lots of bundles available, each filled with clues related to the bundle theme!

  2. Debunk the Clue

    Each clue correlates to a building that is located in places related to the bundle!

  3. Go to the Location

    Make your way to the building you think the clue correlates to!

  4. Check In

    Click the check in button under each clue to see if you made it to the correct location! You get 5 attempts for each clue!

  5. Score Points

    The amount of points you earn for each clue decreases the more attempts you use! Hints have no impact on your score. Check out the leaderboard to see where you place!

  6. Top the Leaderboard

    Check out the leaderboard to see where you place among your schoolmates!
